Who We Are

Our Journey

We started PixelBuzz with a simple belief: that every wedding is a story waiting to be told beautifully. Based in Hazaribagh, we serve couples across Jharkhand who value artistry, authenticity, and cinematic quality in their wedding memories.

What began as a passion for capturing candid moments has evolved into a full-service visual storytelling studio. Over the years, we have honed our craft — blending editorial photography with cinematic filmmaking to create wedding memories that feel alive, even decades later.

Our team brings together diverse creative backgrounds — from fine art photography to documentary filmmaking — united by a shared commitment to excellence. Every frame we capture is intentional, every edit is purposeful, and every story we tell is uniquely yours.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To transform wedding memories into timeless visual stories that couples and families cherish for generations. We believe every celebration deserves to be documented with artistry, care, and an unwavering attention to the moments that matter most.

Our Vision

To become Jharkhand's most trusted name in premium wedding storytelling. We aspire to set new standards in wedding photography and cinematography — where every project reflects creative excellence, emotional depth, and lasting beauty.

Our Approach

Creative Philosophy

Three pillars guide every wedding we document

Emotion First

We prioritize genuine emotion over posed perfection. The stolen glances, the tearful speeches, the unbridled joy — these are the moments that make your wedding story truly yours. Our approach is observational and intuitive, allowing real moments to unfold naturally.

Light as Language

Light is our most powerful storytelling tool. Whether it is the soft golden hour glow, the dramatic interplay of shadows, or the warm flicker of ceremony lamps — we use light to create depth, mood, and visual poetry in every frame.

Timeless Over Trendy

Trends fade, but timeless imagery endures. We deliberately choose classic compositions, refined color palettes, and editorial techniques that will look as stunning in twenty years as they do today. Your wedding deserves a visual legacy, not a passing aesthetic.
